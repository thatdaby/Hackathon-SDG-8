Welcome To Factory Sim!
For the next five days, you'll be the manager of a factory!
[Mantain your factory stats... ]:
- Money must be above $500! (must be above or equal to $3000 to win!)
- Production Level must be above 10%! (must be above or equal to 75% to win!)
- Worker Morale must be above 10% (Don't make them sad now!) (must be above or equal to 75% to win!)
- Factory Safety must be above 20%
...to keep the factory up and running!
Every event gives you passive income. This scales with morale and production level. Keep this in mind!
Each day will have 4 events, your choices will either increase or decrease a stat.
Have fun!
